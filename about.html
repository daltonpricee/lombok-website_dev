Who We Are

Lombok Global School is founded to meet the clear demand for quality international education in South Lombok. Families here have long recognized the need for a school that provides both global standards and a supportive community environment.

Our Vision

We aspire to raise socially responsible students by connecting ideas and cultures globally through a learning environment where children can thrive academically, emotionally, socially, and ethically. We aim to be the school of choice in South Lombok, delivering internationally recognized education.

Our Mission

We nurture resilient global citizens in an inclusive community, grounded in rigorous teaching, inquiry, and a commitment to both success and service.

Our Values

Resilience, Curiosity, Excellence, Service, and Community.
